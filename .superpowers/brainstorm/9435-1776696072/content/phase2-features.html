Phase 2 — What gets added?

Persistence + Report History turns AgriLife into a real app. Here's what's included:

DATABASE (H2 embedded)

Zero config. Ships inside the JAR. Every form submission saved automatically with timestamp, farmer name, crop, pest, pesticide, delivery time.

HISTORY PAGE /reports

List of all past submissions — newest first. Each row: farmer name, crop, pest, pesticide, delivery time, date. Click row to expand full detail.
